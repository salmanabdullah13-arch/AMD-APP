Design Notes

Key decisions, open questions, ideas

Operations
Variations are NOT raised by Operations — only by Sales or Production Managers. Operations is read-only, just tracks status and sends reminders if stuck.
Today
Curtain & Blinds
Dedicated install team separate from workshop. Sequence: Site measure → Fabric order → Arrives → Cut & sew → Track fitting → QC → Install → Sign-off. Client signs physical form on site.
Today
BOM Ownership
3 modes per dept: (1) Wait for PM — 48h deadline, (2) Operations fills directly, (3) Delegate to any staff with deadline + alert. Flexibility is key.
Today
Budget Approval
Under BD 5,000 — Operations approves immediately. Over BD 5,000 — escalates to Owner (Salman) before production starts. Production is on hold until approved.
Today
Mixed Projects
One quote auto-splits to department work-orders. Departments only converge at final delivery. Each dept PM sets their own delivery date. Project tracker watches convergence.
Today
Accounts
Tally stays for accounting. Bridge needed so invoices don't need double entry. Q-Pro may or may not have an API — check with Nettworksy first.
